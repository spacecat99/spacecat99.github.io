Project overview

My good friend is starting her own small business, so i offered to help with her online campaign. This project was different from the others, because for these designs i had an actual costumer that gave some guidelines to follow. And after first suggestions, i immediately got an honest feedback from her and concrete wishes that had to be incorporated into end product. So i didn't go through the usability studies as per usual, because the only person checking the progress was the client herself.

The Goal

Her wish was a simple one site Webpage with basic animation and not a lot of interaction. She wanted to go sure that the site is wellcoming for users of all different ages. costumers have to be able to get the most important information as fast as they can. The information has to be clear, not hidden in long texts. also not overshodowed by any other element of teh site, such as unrelated pictures and animations. The color palete and the design should be appeling and conforting to the eye but also awake an interest.

My Role

Single UX designer leading the app and responsive website design from conception to delivery Conducting interviews (family members and friends), paper and digital wireframing, low and high-fidelity prototyping, conducting usability studies (family members and friends), iterating on designs, determining information architecture, and responsive design.
