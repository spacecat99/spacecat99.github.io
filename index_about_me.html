my path

I was born and raised in a small country in Central Europe, Slovenia. At the age of 16, I moved with my family to Switzerland, so my parents could fulfill their business opportunities. Shortly after that, I got into highschool in Lucerne and graduated from it in summer 2020. I wasn't very decisive about which career path should I take.
I’m a very curious person, with lots of interest in different areas and professions. But visual Design and Animation were always the strongest passions of mine, so I started to educate myself and practice in my free time with many different softwares like Blender, Photoshop, Illustrator, Clip studio, Adobe Animate...
After time, I discovered UX Design that really grabbed my attention. I attended 'Google UX Design Course' for a year and learned so much about the workflow, thought process and the execution process. I did some programming courses as well, to expand my programming skills and to be able to code in Java, JS, Python, Pascal and the obvious HTML and CSS.
In the meantime I started my studies on the University in Hagen where I chose the bachelor of science Computer Science, to combine my designing skills and love for animation with theoretic and practical knowledge of Informatics.

So here I am now. Showing you what I’ve learned so far and constantly updating my newest projects.
